5 Years of CIHR Funding

This interactive visualization depicts the amount of CIHR funding awarded to each Canadian province over the last 5 fiscal years.

The total dollar value of the funding alloted to each province is normalized by population, and expressed in CAD.

This visualization was created for a group of researchers at the Michael Smith Foundation, and is intended to be viewed on a full screen, which you may do here. For more information about the Canadian Institute for Health Research (CIHR), click here.

The map above depicts the per-capita CIHR funding allotted to each Canadian province in each of the last 5 fiscal years.

This map is drawn at a scale of roughly 1:20,000,000, but this value changes depending on the size of your browser window.

The bar chart at left shows the total value of CIHR funding allotted to each of the 5 highest winning provinces. The value is summed over the last 5 fiscal years.

The data used in this visualization comes from the Canadian Institutes of Health Research (CIHR). The dataset can be found here.
